Cardio

Grappling burns a lot of calories because of its aerobic and anerobic demands. Most gyms implement a type of sparring (known as rolling) where members of a gym can practice techniques with students of all levels and sizes. Rounds go for 5 minutes and can be as much as 30 minutes of the class.

Strength

In addition to good heart health, increase in strength can also occur. Jiu Jitsu incorporates many joint locks and submissions that require control of one's body and exerting strength to control another.

Mobility

As we age, our bodies naturally become less mobile. Regular practice of jiu jitsu puts your body in many different positions which helps maintain flexibility. The consistency of training demands movements that keeps your body loose.
